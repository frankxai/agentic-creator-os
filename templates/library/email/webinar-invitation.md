## Conversion Optimization

### Increase Registrations
- Emphasize "free" in subject line
- Lead with biggest benefit
- Use social proof
- Create urgency (limited spots)
- Offer valuable bonus
- Make registration easy (1-click)

### Increase Attendance
- Multiple reminder emails
- Calendar integration
- Day-before prep email
- 1-hour reminder with link
- SMS reminder (if available)
- Emphasize live-only bonuses

### Increase Engagement
- Encourage questions beforehand
- Interactive elements during webinar
- Q&A session
- Polls and surveys
- Chat participation
- Post-webinar community

---

## Performance Metrics

### Target Benchmarks
- **Registration rate**: 20-40% of email list
- **Attendance rate**: 30-50% of registrants
- **Engagement rate**: 60%+ stay until end
- **Replay views**: 20-30% of registrants

### Optimization
- Test different days/times
- A/B test webinar topics
- Vary email send times
- Segment by interest
- Personalize based on behavior
- Follow up with non-attendees

---

## Best Practices

### Timing
- **Invitation**: 7-14 days before
- **Reminder 1**: 3-5 days before
- **Reminder 2**: 1 day before
- **Reminder 3**: 1 hour before
- **Replay**: Within 24 hours

### Day/Time
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Best attendance
- **11 AM or 2 PM**: Optimal times (local)
- **Avoid Mondays/Fridays**: Lower attendance
- **Consider timezones**: Multiple sessions if global

### Duration
- **30-45 minutes**: Ideal length
- **60 minutes max**: Including Q&A
- **Respect time**: Start and end on time
