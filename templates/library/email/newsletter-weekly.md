## Newsletter Formatting Tips

### Structure
- **Scannable**: Use headers, bullets, and white space
- **Mobile-first**: 60%+ of emails opened on mobile
- **Single column**: Easier to read on all devices
- **Clear hierarchy**: Most important content first

### Length
- **Ideal**: 200-500 words
- **Max**: 1,000 words
- **Rule**: If it's longer, link to blog post instead

### Links
- **3-5 links** per email (not counting footer)
- **Clear CTAs**: Use buttons for primary actions
- **Link text**: Descriptive, not "click here"

### Personalization
- Use {{FirstName}} in greeting
- Segment by interests when possible
- Reference past interactions
- Conversational tone

## Performance Metrics

### Track These
- **Open rate**: Target 20-30%
- **Click rate**: Target 2-5%
- **Reply rate**: Target 0.5-2%
- **Unsubscribe rate**: Keep under 0.5%
- **Spam complaints**: Keep under 0.1%

### Optimization
- **A/B test**: Subject lines, send times, content order
- **Segment**: By engagement, interests, behavior
- **Clean list**: Remove inactive subscribers quarterly
- **Consistency**: Same day/time each week

---

## Send Time Optimization

### Best Days
1. Tuesday (highest open rates)
2. Thursday
3. Wednesday

### Best Times
- **B2B**: 10 AM - 11 AM (local time)
- **B2C**: 8 PM - 9 PM (local time)
- **Test**: Your audience may differ

### Frequency
- **Weekly**: Most common and effective
- **Bi-weekly**: If you have less content
- **Daily**: Only if highly valuable and expected

## Engagement Boosters

### Interactive Elements
- **Polls**: "Which topic should I cover next?"
- **Questions**: "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
- **Surveys**: Link to quick 1-2 question survey
- **Contests**: Monthly giveaway for engaged subscribers

### Personal Touch
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Share your process
- **Failures**: What didn't work this week
- **Wins**: Celebrate with your community
- **Ask for help**: "I need your input on..."
