## Course Email Sequence

### Email 1: Announcement (Day 1)
- Introduce the course
- Explain the transformation
- Share curriculum overview
- Early bird pricing

### Email 2: Deep Dive (Day 3)
- Detailed module breakdown
- Sample lesson or preview
- Student testimonials
- Address "Is this for me?"

### Email 3: Instructor Story (Day 5)
- Your journey and expertise
- Why you're qualified to teach this
- Your teaching philosophy
- Personal success stories

### Email 4: Student Success (Day 7)
- Multiple case studies
- Before/after transformations
- Specific results
- Common success patterns

### Email 5: FAQ (Day 9)
- Answer common objections
- Technical details
- Time commitment reality
- Support available

### Email 6: Last Chance (Day 11)
- Final reminder
- Urgency (deadline/price increase)
- Quick recap of value
- Strong CTA

## Conversion Optimization

### Overcome Common Objections

**"I don't have time"**
- Emphasize self-paced nature
- Break down weekly time commitment
- Show how to fit it into busy schedule

**"Is this worth the investment?"**
- Calculate ROI
- Compare to alternatives
- Highlight lifetime access
- Emphasize guarantee

**"Can I really learn this?"**
- Share beginner success stories
- Explain support available
- Highlight step-by-step approach
- Offer preview lesson

**"What if I fall behind?"**
- Explain lifetime access
- Describe community support
- Share catch-up strategies
- Emphasize self-paced flexibility
