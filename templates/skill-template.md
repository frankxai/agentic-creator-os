*Tips for writing good skills:*

1. **Be specific**: Vague skills are useless. Include concrete examples.
2. **Be actionable**: Every guideline should lead to an action.
3. **Be honest about scope**: Don't claim the skill does more than it does.
4. **Update regularly**: Skills should evolve with your practice.
5. **Test it**: Use the skill yourself before publishing.
