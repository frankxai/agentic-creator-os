### Task: Design an email sequence
```
5-Email Welcome Sequence: Vibe OS

Email 1: The Arrival (Day 0)
- Subject: "Your first frequency is ready"
- Deliver immediate value (first track)
- Set expectation for journey ahead
- CTA: Listen now

Email 2: The Story (Day 2)
- Subject: "Why I created 500 songs with AI"
- Personal origin story
- Address skepticism about AI music
- CTA: Explore the catalog

Email 3: The Method (Day 4)
- Subject: "The science behind the sound"
- Educational content on frequency/state
- Position as transformation tool, not just music
- CTA: Try a focus session

Email 4: The Proof (Day 6)
- Subject: "What creators are saying"
- Social proof and testimonials
- Address remaining objections
- CTA: Upgrade to full access

Email 5: The Invitation (Day 8)
- Subject: "Ready to transform your creative practice?"
- Direct offer with clear benefits
- Urgency without sleaze
- CTA: Join now
```
