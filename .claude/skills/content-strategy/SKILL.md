# Content Strategy Skill

**Version:** 1.0 (ACOS v11)
**Author:** FrankX
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-15

## Overview

Strategic content planning and creation for the FrankX ecosystem. This skill orchestrates blog articles, newsletters, social content, and documentation with consistent voice and quality.

## Quick Start

```
# Triggers automatically on:
- "write a blog post"
- "create an article"
- "content plan"
- "SEO optimize"
```

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Article Creation
- Long-form blog content (1500-3000 words)
- SEO optimization with keyword research
- Internal linking strategy
- Social snippet extraction

### 2. Content Planning
- Editorial calendar management
- Theme clustering for topical authority
- Content gap analysis
- Repurposing strategies

### 3. Voice Consistency
- Frank DNA enforcement
- AI pattern removal
- Readability optimization (8th-10th grade)
- Quotable insight extraction

## Integration Points

- `/article-creator` - Main article workflow
- `/factory` - Full publishing pipeline
- `/polish-content` - Quality refinement
- `content-polisher` agent - Voice alignment

## Anti-Patterns

- Generic AI-sounding intros ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- Vague claims without evidence
- Missing internal links
- No clear next steps for reader
