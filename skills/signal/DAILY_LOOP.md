# Signal — The Daily Loop

How you run LinkedIn every day with Signal. The goal: **ship one excellent post + ten thoughtful Dream100 comments a day, in under 45 minutes of your time**, because the agents do the preparation and you do the judgment + the click.

Everything drafts; **you post.** That's the ToS-safe design and it's also where your taste enters the loop.

---

## The daily rhythm (≈45 min of your time)

### Morning — Ideate (agents run; you pick) · ~10 min
1. `/linkedin plan` (or `/linkedin research`) — the research swarm sweeps overnight signals (top papers, videos, posts in your lanes) + recalls what performed for you, and returns **5 ranked angles** for today, mapped to your pillars.
2. You pick one (or say "draft all, I'll choose"). 30 seconds.

### Midday — Draft + design (agents run; you edit) · ~20 min
3. `/linkedin draft` — a full post in your chosen voice (personal or company), staged.
4. If it's a carousel/infographic day: `/linkedin carousel` or `/linkedin infographic` — the design agents build slides/visuals from the post via the multimodal + canvas skills, brand-locked.
5. `/linkedin critique` — the independent gate checks voice, claim-honesty, hook strength → PASS / warn / FAIL. You read the draft, make it *yours* (this is the taste step), re-run critique if you changed a lot.

### Afternoon — Distribute + engage (agents draft; you click) · ~15 min
6. `/linkedin ship` — exports the passed post to a Typefully/LinkedIn **draft**. You review and **hit post** (or schedule it there).
7. `/linkedin engage` — surfaces your Dream100's newest posts and drafts a substantive comment on each. You skim, tweak, **post the good ones** (5–10/day). This is the fastest growth lever under ~5k followers.

### Evening (optional, 2 min)
8. `/linkedin learn` — paste the day's/yesterday's post metrics (or let the analytics pull run). Signal writes the outcome to the store so tomorrow's `plan` is grounded in what's actually working for *you*, not generic best-practice.

---

## Weekly cadence (Sunday, ~30 min)

- `/linkedin plan --week` — lay out the week across your pillars in the Notion calendar (a hypothesis; the daily loop refines it).
- Target mix (starting point, `learn` corrects it): ~40% insight/opinion · ~30% proof/build-in-public · ~20% repurpose-with-credit · ~10% story.
- Refresh the Dream100 (add 2–3, drop any that never engage back).
- Review last week's `learn` deltas: which hooks/formats/times won → feed next week.

---

## The compounding claim (why this gets better, not just faster)

Every shipped post's real performance flows back through `/linkedin learn` into the store. Within a few weeks, `plan` stops guessing and starts recommending *your* proven hooks, *your* best times, *your* highest-converting formats. That loop — not the drafting speed — is what carries you to Top Voice. It's the [Excellence Loop](../../docs/architecture/EXCELLENCE_LOOP.md) applied to LinkedIn.
