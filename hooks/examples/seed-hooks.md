## FOUNDATION TIER

### Research-Backed Hooks

#### Hook R001: The Focus Study
**Dimension:** Research
**Target Emotion:** Recognition
**Best Platform:** YouTube, LinkedIn

- **Visual Hook:** Split screen: distracted multitasker vs. single-task deep worker
- **Audio Hook:** Notification sounds chaos → silence → focused typing
- **Text Hook:** "Cal Newport's research: Deep work produces 4x output."

**Why It Works:** Combines credible source with specific multiplier that challenges common behavior.

---

#### Hook R002: The Pattern Analysis
**Dimension:** Research
**Target Emotion:** Curiosity
**Best Platform:** Twitter/X, TikTok

- **Visual Hook:** Rapid montage of different creators working, then freeze frame highlighting one common element
- **Audio Hook:** Upbeat analysis music, then pause, then "Found it."
- **Text Hook:** "After studying 1,000 creators, one pattern appeared in 94%."

**Why It Works:** Large sample size implies rigor; specific percentage creates curiosity gap.

## EXPANSION TIER

### Counter-Intuitive Hooks

#### Hook CI001: The Disproven Rule
**Dimension:** Counter-Intuitive
**Target Emotion:** Curiosity
**Best Platform:** TikTok, YouTube Shorts

- **Visual Hook:** "10,000 Hours" text appearing then cracking/shattering
- **Audio Hook:** Confident statement, then record scratch, then "Actually..."
- **Text Hook:** "The research that disproved 10,000 hours."

**Why It Works:** Challenges well-known belief with implied authority; viral potential high.

#### Hook CI002: The Paradox
**Dimension:** Counter-Intuitive
**Target Emotion:** Wonder
**Best Platform:** LinkedIn, Twitter/X

- **Visual Hook:** Person stepping back from work, then showing better output appearing
- **Audio Hook:** Stress sounds releasing, then creative flow music
- **Text Hook:** "Working less produced more. Here's the math."

**Why It Works:** Direct paradox creates cognitive dissonance; "the math" promises logic.

### Adventure Hooks

**Dimension:** Humor
**Target Emotion:** Amusement


#### Hook H002: The Anti-Advice
**Dimension:** Humor
**Target Emotion:** Recognition
**Best Platform:** Twitter/X, Instagram

- **Visual Hook:** Serious "life advice" aesthetic, then reveal absurd content
- **Audio Hook:** Inspirational music that continues earnestly through punchline
- **Text Hook:** "The productivity hack that changed my life: lowering my standards."

**Why It Works:** Subverts expectations; truth in jest lands; earnest delivery makes it funnier.

## Dimension Selection Guide

| If your content is about... | Use Dimension |
|-----------------------------|---------------|
| Data, studies, proven findings | Research |
| Underlying structures, systems | First Principles |
| Expert insights, earned wisdom | Wisdom |
| Challenging assumptions | Counter-Intuitive |
| Personal journey, pivots | Transformation |
| Shared discovery, journey | Adventure |
| Relatable absurdity, wit | Humor |
| Future trends, emerging tech | Visionary |
| Physics, cosmos, deep science | Frontier Science |
| Depth, unknown territories | Exploration |

## Usage

1. **Pick your dimension** — Match to your content's core nature
2. **Adapt the formula** — Keep the structure, change the specifics
3. **Match to platform** — Adjust pacing and format
4. **Test and iterate** — Track what works for your audience
5. **Contribute back** — Add successful hooks to expand the library
